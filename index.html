Privacy Policy for Autofy

Effective Date: 01 February 2026

Autofy, formerly known as AutoPartsSL, is operated by Vihan Mapalagama (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Autofy mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Vehicle and Listing Information

  • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • Auto part details, images, and descriptions submitted by users
  • Messages exchanged within the platform

Location Information

If you use features such as accident reporting or roadside assistance, we may collect your device location while the feature is actively in use. Location data is not collected in the background.

Usage and Diagnostic Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic reports
  • Basic usage information to improve reliability and performance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable listing and discovery of auto parts
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Improve application performance and stability
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase for database and backend infrastructure
  • Firebase for hosting infrastructure

These providers process data on our behalf and are required to protect your information. We do not sell your personal data.

4. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission and secure infrastructure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Autofy is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
