Privacy Policy for AutoPartsSL

Effective Date: 01 February 2026

AutoPartsSL, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the AutoPartsSL mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Vehicle and Listing Information

  • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • Auto part details, images, and descriptions submitted by users

Usage Information

  • Basic app usage data to improve reliability and performance

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable listing and discovery of auto parts
  • Allow communication between buyers and sellers
  • Improve app functionality
  • Provide customer support

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers supporting hosting or analytics, or when required by law.

Location data is used to tag accident locations and enable roadside assistance services. Location data is accessed only during active use of these features and is not tracked continuously.

Data Security

Data is encrypted during transmission and protected using standard security measures.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Users may request deletion by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

AutoPartsSL is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will appear on this page.
